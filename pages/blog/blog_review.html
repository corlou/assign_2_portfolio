Product Review: Sun Bum

It was the only brand on the shelf and I was heading out for a day walking around the city and needed something... anything to protect me from the Brisbane summer sun.

"Trust the Bum" it said. I chuckled. Alright then. So I decided to play along and see what the fuss was about. WELL. Let me tell you. I have thrown out all of my other sunscreens because this is the best one. No longer do I feel sticky or smell weird after applying a coat or two and I have yet to get burned after following the instructions of how to apply.

I may have gone a bit overboard when I discovered their hair care products too. Now my hair smells like banana. Not the heavily fake banana candy. No. The light, fresh, beachy smell. I smell liek summer time and I love it.

Pros:

  • Smells incredible
  • Vegan
  • Cruelty Free
  • Silicone Free
  • Paraben Free
  • UV-Protecting

Cons:

  • Hard to find in stores
