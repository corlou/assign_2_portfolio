Self-Care Sundays

Despite being able to work and study full time, I need to take a break at least once a week. All of my days were filled with work, study, partners, cat, lyra class, and gym.

I lasted three solid weeks. Not too bad really. But my break down came in the form of not being able to get out of my car and attend a work training session. I was having hysterics and anxiety in the car and barely pulled it together to walk to the front door.

I walked up to the organiser and said "I'm sorry but my mental health is so shit right now. I need out." I went home and slept for hours. I ate what I wanted and watched tv.

Now on every Sunday, I have a rule where I don't touch my laptop. I'm not even allowed to sit at my desk in our home office. I go out and do things. I go to the beach, the markets, the op-shops. I do exactly what I want to do and I enjoy myself immensly.

I come back to work on Mondays, refreshed and ready to handle whatever the week throws my way. It does help that I have a very supportive workplace and friends too. So have a day. Do what you want. Wear what you want. Just enjoy this precious time we have.
