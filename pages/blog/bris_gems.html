Hidden Gems in Brisbane

Suburban Social Chapel Hill

A cute little place for lunch and dinner that turns magical around sunset. There's plently of parking and they make excellent food and cocktails. They also seem to have a variety of wacky beers on tap every time I visit.

Place of Stones

Now don't let it's simplicity fool you this place serves some of the best Italian food I've had in a long time. It's not licensed so BYO and the staff are so lovely that I feel right at home.

Proud Henry

Tucked away in Fortitude Valley, this cute little gin bar is great for a quiet drink. Sit out the back in a small garden with candles and fairy lights and you wouldn't feel like you're in the middle of party town. Their range is extensive and the knowledge is deep. If you don't know what you want,. ask for somethign fruity, spicy, or herbal and you won't be disappointed.
